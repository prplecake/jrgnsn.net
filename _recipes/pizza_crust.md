---
layout: recipe
title: Pizza Crust
tagline: "Super Simple Pizza Crust"
ingredients:
  - pizza crust:
    - name: 'flour'
      qty: 1-1/4 cup
    - name: yeast
      qty: 1-1/8 tsp
    - name: sugar
      qty: 1 tsp
    - name: salt
      qty: 1/8 tsp
    - name: olive oil
      qty: 1 tbsp
    - name: water
      qty: 1/2 cup
source: My brother Phil
---

1. Combine ingredients and let sit for 5 minutes.
2. Roll onto greased sheet and add toppings.
3. Bake for 15 minutes or until crust is golden brown.
